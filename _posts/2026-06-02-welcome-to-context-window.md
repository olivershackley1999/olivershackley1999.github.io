This is **Context Window** — a technical blog about Microsoft AI and the modern
way of working: M365 Copilot, Copilot Studio, Agents 365, Cowork, and the
occasional detour into developer territory (Foundry and friends).

The bias here is toward *mechanism over hype*. Less "what it's called," more
"what it does, why it does it, and what breaks when it's missing." If a post
can't tell you why something works, it isn't finished.

## Why "Context Window"

It's the literal thing that bounds what a model can see at once — the working
memory of every system on this blog. It also happens to describe the goal:
giving you enough *context* to reason about Microsoft's AI stack instead of
memorizing acronyms.

## What to expect

- Architecture walk-throughs that explain the gears, not just the diagram.
- Practical notes from real customer and deployment scenarios.
- Honest takes — what's solid, what's half-baked, what's just a demo.
