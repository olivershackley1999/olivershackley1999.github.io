**Context Window** is where I write about Microsoft's AI solutions — not just how they work, but how they actually get used at work.

I'm Oliver Shackley, a Cloud Solution Architect at Microsoft. Most posts here are born out of a real customer conversation or a deployment: M365 Copilot, Copilot Studio, Agents 365, Foundry, and the occasional developer topic.

I care less about what a feature is called and more about how it works, why it works that way, and what breaks when a piece is missing.

This is an independent site. Views are my own and it is not affiliated with Microsoft.
